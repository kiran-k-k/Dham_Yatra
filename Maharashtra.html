6 Famous Temples of Maharashtra

Shree Siddhivinayak Temple.

Mumbai.

In the heart of Mumbai, the Siddhivinayak Temple is a haven of peace and spirituality devoted to the venerated god Ganesha. Devotees from all over the world come here in search of blessings and heavenly grace because of its majestic architecture and calm atmosphere.

Shirdi Sai Baba Temple.

Shirdi.

The Shirdi Sai Baba Temple is a spiritual sanctuary devoted to the venerated saint Sai Baba, and it is tucked away in the charming town of Shirdi. Enveloped in mystique and reverence, this sacred abode exudes an aura of peace, attracting devotees of spiritual enlightenment.

Trimbakeshwar Temple.

Nashik.

Trimbakeshwar Temple, nestled amidst the scenic beauty of Nashik, is a sacred shrine dedicated to Lord Shiva. With its intricate architecture and spiritual significance, it serves as a pilgrimage site for devotees seeking blessings and divine intervention.

Mahalakshmi Temple.

Kolhapur.

Mahalakshmi Temple, situated in the historic city of Kolhapur, is a revered shrine dedicated to Goddess Mahalakshmi, the goddess of wealth and prosperity. Its ornate architecture and spiritual aura attract devotees seeking blessings and solace.

Grishneshwar Temple.

Ellora.

Grishneshwar Temple, nestled amidst the UNESCO World Heritage Site of Ellora Caves, is a sacred abode dedicated to Lord Shiva. Renowned for its architectural splendour and spiritual significance, it attracts devotees and history enthusiasts alike.

Bhimashankar Jyotirlinga Temple.

Bhimashankar.

Situated amidst the lush Sahyadri ranges, Bhimashankar Temple is one of the 12 Jyotirlingas devoted to Lord Shiva. This ancient shrine offers devotees a soul-stirring experience amidst tranquil surroundings, which brings serenity to oneself.
